Information

  1. Press the button q on key board to close the opencv window.
  2. Virtual Paint
    • Use a pointer in order to paint on the window
    • The pointing device should be of blue colour to get detected
    • Bring the pointing device to the colour you want to choose for painting
    • If you want to clear the window bring the pointing device on the clear tab
  3. Sudoku Solver
    • Place the sudoku in front of the camera
    • Please keep the sudoku still for correct output
    • A sudoku can be presented from a mobile phone or on a paper
  4. Steganography
    • Please select a image you want to encrypt your message into by clicking on choose file
    • Please write your message to be encrypted in the text box
    • For decryption please choose a encrypted image
    • The message will appear on the image after decryption
